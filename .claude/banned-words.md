# banned-words.md — résumé content exclusion list (check every generated bullet against this)

Purpose: keep résumé bullets from reading as AI-generated. Cut the vague verbs/adjectives below (any
tense/plural/`-ing`/`-er`/`-ive` form). This does **not** mean cutting ATS keywords — tool names, cert
names, exact job-title phrases, and technical skills are *nouns* and stay. See
`../reference/resume-writing-rules.md → Bullet writing`.

## Verbs (banned)
delve · leverage · utilize · harness · streamline · underscore · foster · spearhead *(unless literally
led a team)* · champion · facilitate · cultivate · drive *(as in "drive results"; "AI-driven" → use
"AI-powered"/"AI-based" or name the mechanism)* · unlock · unleash · empower · elevate · optimize
*(unless it is the literal technical action taken)* · synergize · orchestrate · pioneer

## Adjectives (banned)
robust · seamless · cutting-edge · innovative · dynamic · comprehensive · holistic · versatile ·
results-driven · detail-oriented · proven · strategic *(as a filler modifier)* · meticulous · adept ·
tech-savvy · game-changing · groundbreaking · transformative · impactful · pivotal · crucial · paramount

## Nouns (banned)
synergy · paradigm · landscape · realm · tapestry · ecosystem *(unless literally a technical system)* ·
journey · cornerstone · testament

## Transitions / openers (banned)
furthermore · moreover · additionally *(as a bullet opener)* · notably · importantly · it's worth noting

## Résumé clichés (banned)
"results-driven professional" · "proven track record" · "strong collaborator" · "wear many hats" ·
"hit the ground running" · "self-starter" · "team player" · "go-getter" · "extensive experience in"

## Also banned (punctuation / openers — see the rules doc)
- Em dashes (—) and en dashes (–) as sentence breaks → use a period, comma, or plain colon.
- Semicolons joining two independent clauses in a bullet.
- Colons used for a dramatic reveal ("The result: …").
- Starting a bullet with "Successfully," "Effectively," or "Proactively."
