# Prep-Doc — Lumen Retail · Lead Product Designer

**Hook (line 1):** "I move retail funnels with research, not opinions — I've cut onboarding drop-off
41%→24% and lifted checkout completion 18%, and I grow the designers around me while I do it."

## Role thesis (why I win this loop)
Lumen wants a Lead IC who owns a consumer-retail surface's design quality, lifts conversion, and mentors
a pod — explicitly not a people manager. That's the exact shape of my consumer 0→1 and funnel work
(Atlas) plus my mentoring practice (Beacon), with retail range from agency self-checkout work.

## Talking points (expanded)
1. **0→1 onboarding (E5 / M5–M6).** 41%→24% drop-off across ~40k signups — full ownership,
   research-through-ship.
2. **Conversion funnel (E6 / M7).** Checkout completion +18% — directly retail-relevant.
3. **Research depth (E7 / M10).** 130+ moderated sessions; I design with evidence.
4. **Mentorship (E9 / M9).** 3–4 designers, structured crits — the "Lead" mentoring this role wants.
5. **Retail range (E8).** Self-checkout pilot — domain credibility without overclaiming recency.

  build models."
