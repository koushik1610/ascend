# Interview Questions — Lumen Retail · Lead Product Designer

## (a) Technical / domain / design questions (~13)
1. **Walk me through a funnel you moved.** → Atlas checkout +18% / onboarding 41%→24% · variants,
   tested, shipped · artifact: D2 / E6.
2. **How do you diagnose a drop-off?** → Funnel read + qual (where vs. why); the 3 activation cliffs
   framing · artifact: D2.
3. **How do you validate before shipping?** → 3 prototyped variants, 8–10 usability sessions each,
   task-success + time-to-complete · artifact: D2.
4. **Design a better checkout for [Lumen surface].** → Clarify goal/metric → IA → friction removal →
   trust/clarity → test plan · artifact: E6.
5. **How do you balance conversion with brand/experience?** → Optimize for the user's task; conversion
   follows clarity, not dark patterns.
6. **How do you work with data without a data background?** → Read funnels, co-design experiments, mix
   quant+qual; data *partner* not modeler · honesty boundary · artifact: D3 / S5.
7. **Tell me about a 0→1 problem.** → Atlas onboarding from blank canvas · artifact: S1 / D2.
8. **How do you mentor as an IC?** → Structured crit, weekly pairing, model by going first · artifact:
   S4.
9. **A time research changed the plan.** → Onboarding: long expectations screen tested worse, cut to
   just-in-time · artifact: D2 limitation.
10. **How do you prioritize a roadmap with limited research?** → Lightweight cadence, impact ×
    addressability · artifact: E7.
11. **Mobile vs. web funnel differences?** → Thumb reach, interruption, context; rebuilt rider mobile
    flow · artifact: E6.
12. **Retail-specific: in-store + digital handoff?** → Self-checkout pilot framing · artifact: E8 ·
    honest about recency.
13. **How do you measure your own success?** → Funnel movement + research-validated decisions + the
    designers I grow.
