# Interview Packet — structure

Build these files in `interview-packet/`. Each piece of content lives here ONCE; per-job folders
reference it by ID. Fill every `<…>` with the user's real, defensible material — never invent events.

---

## `star-stories.md`
6–10 behavioral stories in STAR form, each with a stable ID. Cover the dimensions senior loops probe.

### S1 — <theme, e.g., ambiguity / zero prior art>
- **S:** <situation>
- **T:** <task>
- **A:** <action — the decisions, not just activity>
- **R:** <result, quantified where honest>
- **Proves:** <the signal it sends; which audience it leads for>

*(S2…S10 — same shape. Themes to cover: influence without authority · leverage/scale · technical
conviction under pushback · coaching/multiplying · conflict/disagreement · operational excellence ·
genuine failure-and-learning.)*
